A Muse Me is a clothing brand where I built and refined a client’s visual identity and created presentation-ready marketing assets. The goal was to make the logo capture the feeling of an “endless Italian summer” into a cohesive brand language suitable for digital campaigns and client pitches.

  • Designed and refined logo colour system in Illustrator.
  • Created high-fidelity pitch deck and product mockups in Photoshop.
  • Iterated with the client to align visuals with their target audience.

The branding started by finding inspirations for the client’s vision. Together we picked out brands such as WITH LOVE, Love and Flair, and Love, Bonito for their aesthetics, tone, and marketing.

I started by developing a moodboard to find color palettes that suited the client’s needs along with multiple logo variations. The client needed a cohesive identity that could translate across social media, packaging, and pitch decks.

Once the colour palette and logo were finalized, I produced high-quality visual mockups in Photoshop. I designed business cards, clothing tags, and shopping bags to help the client visualize how the branding would function in real-world marketing contexts.

This project highlights my strengths in brand refinement, digital marketing asset creation, iterative design, and client-focused communication.
