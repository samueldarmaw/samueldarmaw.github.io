Bio

I’m Samuel Sutantra Darmawangsa, a UI/UX and graphic designer focused on how interfaces guide attention, timing, and flow. Inspired by film editing and visual storytelling, I design experiences with clear hierarchy, intentional transitions, and interactions that feel effortless rather than overwhelming.

I aim to build interfaces that feel invisible: the next action is obvious, feedback is immediate, and nothing competes for attention. I work primarily in Figma and Adobe tools, and I care deeply about what actually ships.

I’m currently looking for opportunities where I can design end-to-end experiences, contribute to design systems, and turn complex ideas into simple, satisfying products.
